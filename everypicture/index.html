Laura's top 5 boba places

Davis Edition

T4 #5

T4 was my favorite boba place freshman year. The drinks were the cheap, consistent, and I could get my favorite roasted oolong milk tea. It also had bonus points for being close to Chipotle. However, now the drinks are more expensive for about the same quality-which is okay, but not worth spending $6+. Other places are now higher on this list.

Reasons to Go

  • Good classic drinks
  • Snacks are pretty good
  • Lots of seating

Reasons to Stay Home

  • Raised their prices but the quality and quantity are the same
  • Harder to park
